Who am I?

In one word: curious. It took me a study abroad experience in the Netherlands and four changed majors to find my life passion of cartography. Since then, my love for cartography, geography, and data visualization has continued to grow. I will graduate in Spring 2020 with a bachelor's degree at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Graduate school will be my next adventure as I seek to study visual storytelling. I am interested in how cartography and visual storytelling can come together to achieve more memorable, just, and accessible stories. You can learn more by checking out my Resume or a PDF of my CV.

In my free time I do a lot of biking, reading of fantasy books, and building my own LEGO creations. I also love to travel outside of the United States. I have learned so much from studying abroad in Nicaragua, Denmark, and the Netherlands. I hope to continue traveling as I have never been to Australia, Africa, Asia, or South America.
