I have always been a curious person. As I grew up, my academic interests continued to expand. As an undergraduate, I changed majors four times before finding my niche in cartography/GIS. What I love most about the field of study is all it has to offer such as the following: Math, programming, analysis, design, communication, collaboration, creativity, travel, and much more. Further, the subject of maps never remains static. I can dabble in each of the fields that interest me including. Environmental science, human geography, horticulture, history, and cultural geography. I see mapping as a science, an art, and a way to discover something about myself and the world I live in.
