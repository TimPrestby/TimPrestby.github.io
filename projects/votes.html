The political atmosphere was chaotic when I designed this graphic as the impeachment inquiries for Trump were starting up. Since I never made a map grappling with politics before, I knew this was a great time to reflect on the previous election results.

This graphic was a collaboration between me and a political science student. I needed some inspiration for a graphic and reached out to him. He guided me on the ethical considerations rooted in political science. I created the maps, refined the data in excel, and added storytelling elements.

Created using Indiemapper, Illustrator and Excel
