Milwaukee: Segregated Isolation

I was honored to receive the Hilldale Undergraduate Fellowship in 2019 to help fund research by Professor Song Gao and I investigating neighborhood isolation. The visualization is the original design featured in the journal Environment and Planning A. The map is changed for the paper to meet the requests of the editor.

I collaborated with Yuhao Kang, Joe App, and Professor Song Gao on this project. I did the visualization, storytelling, and some data wrangling. Accordingly, I wrote scripts to extract demographic and mobility data from our database. Once I processed the data, I brought it into illustrator and tweaked transparency features until the flows rose to figure. Yuhao managed the database of Safegraph data while Joe programmed the community detection model in R. Professor Song Gao helped us frame and develop the research methodology as he had the idea for the research subject.

Created using QGIS, R, Pandas, and Python
