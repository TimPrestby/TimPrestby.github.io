Milwaukee: Segregated Isolation

I was honored to receive the Hilldale Undergraduate Fellowship in 2019 to help fund research by Professor Song Gao and me investigating neighborhood isolation. The visualization is the original design featured in the journal Environment and Planning A. The map is changed for the paper to meet the requests of the editor.

I collaborated with three other people on this project. I did the visualization, storytelling, and data wrangling. Accordingly, I wrote scripts to extract demographic and mobility data from our database. Once I processed the data, I brought it into illustrator and tweaked transparency features until the flows rose to figure.

Created using QGIS, R, Pandas, and Python
