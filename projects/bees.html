Bee Aware

I cultivated a deeper passion for sustainable food systems having worked on an organic vegetable farm for over two years and having lived in the Greenhouse Learning Community at UW-Madison. This project is a tribute to the amazing work that bees do and a plea to help save the bees.

This longform infographic is my most in-depth visualization which integrated visual storytelling concepts. A coherent color palette is used throughout to reinforce the mood. Continuity is supported by having the path of the bee character follow the reader through the infographic as the user scrolls.

Created using Illustrator and Indiemapper
