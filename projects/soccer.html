Penn State Soccer Jerseys

For this project, I worked with PSU Athletics to curate some spatial data for the Men's and Women's Soccer Jerseys. The white jersey highlights the terrain of the State College Area using contour lines. The dark jerseys feature the road network in State College, PA.
