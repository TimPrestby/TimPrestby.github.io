Sheridan Park Coastal Erosion

As a kid, science was always my favorite subject. Particularly, I was drawn to natural processes of climate, weather, and hydrology. My dreams of doing a mapping project rooted in science came to fruiton as I got involved with the UW-Seagrant.

I learned how to work in ESRI Storymaps during this project. The time required to make this web map was a lot less than any other web-based map since no programming was involved. This was also my first project which involved collaboration with non-cartographers. I really enjoyed working with engineers and coastal scientists because of the rich domainology they offered along with their help in authoring the story.

Created using ArcGIS StoryMaps
